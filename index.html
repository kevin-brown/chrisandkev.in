What to Wear?

Please dress in comfortable cocktail attire. As the reception goes into the night and we're encouraging star gazing, it's advised to bring a coat or sweater!

Outside or Inside?

The ceremony will be outside at the Gazebo, the ceremony will be inside with access to the Ballroom courtyard - it's the night of the Perseid Meteor shower so be ready to see some shooting stars!

Are kids allowed?

As we're going to be having an open bar for the Cocktail Hour and the wedding runs until midnight. As such, we're asking you to leave children at home.

Accomidations?

There are several nearby hotels that should have availabilities on Friday evening. Please consider booking earlier as opposed to later.


Can I take Photos?

The ceremony is going to be unplugged, but feel free to take as many photos as you want during the reception! Feel free to post them with the hashtag #ChrisandKevInLove

What time to Arrive?

The ceremony will begin at 6:30, and we recommend arriving 15-30 minutes early to get a good seat.

Open Bar?

We have a hosted bar during cocktail hour, and after that during the reception it will be a Cash Bar.

Other Questions?

Feel free to send Chris an Email  or Message if you have any other questions.
